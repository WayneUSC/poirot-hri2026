# Questionnaire Summary

The complete wording appears in `paper/POIROT-HRI2026-appendix.pdf`. This file summarizes the measurement blocks for replication planning.

## Narrative Immersion Scale

15 items, 7-point agreement scale. The appendix reports high internal consistency and a dominant single-factor structure.

## Social Presence Scale

5 items, 7-point agreement scale. Items measure whether the robot game master felt present, attentive, socially responsive, and acceptable as a game host.

## Game Experience and Robot Interaction Items

Items 1-7 were administered to all participants. Items 8-11 were administered only in the Physical-Card condition because they refer to robot-mediated tangible clue delivery.

## Moderator and Background Measures

The study also measured negative attitudes toward robots, Big Five personality traits, demographics, and prior murder-mystery/game experience.
